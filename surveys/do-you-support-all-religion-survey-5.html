Survey 5: Do you support religions? (Excluding yours)

Posted on May 8, 2025

📊 Survey 5 Summary

  • 87.5% | 🇦 Yes
  • 12.5% | 🇧 No
  • 0.0% | 🇨 I try to
  • 0.0% | 🇩 I hate them
  • 0.0% | 🇪 I hate all religions

The results show a strong sense of interfaith respect and tolerance. A significant majority—87.5%—of respondents confirmed that they support other religions apart from their own.

Interestingly, no votes were cast for more extreme negative responses, such as hatred towards other or all religions. This suggests that respondents either hold neutral or open-minded perspectives.

Only 12.5% selected “No,” indicating a limited opposition, possibly due to personal experiences, cultural rigidity, or misunderstanding. This emphasizes the need for more awareness, dialogue, and educational initiatives to bridge gaps.

Overall, this survey reflects a society leaning toward harmony and mutual respect in religious matters, which is a positive sign in today’s divided world.
