Survey 1: If a bully is bullying someone, do you try to stop them or just watch the person getting bullied suffer?

Posted on April 29, 2025

Survey 1 Summary:

When they see someone being bullied, 57.1% of the participants reported that they intervene and completely halt the bully, while 42.9% report confronting and fighting the bully in order to get them to stop. No one reported remaining silent because they were afraid or giving up after attempting. This indicates that most individuals wish to take firm action when they observe bullying occur.
